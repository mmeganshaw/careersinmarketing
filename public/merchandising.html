Merchandising

A Day in the Life

The day starts off by first checking your email to see if marketing or any retail locations have reached out in regards to new or current programs. After going through email, you begin to go through your To-Do list, making sure that the correct products appear in the right stores, and are outlined on the website properly, with the correct inventory being shown. After reviewing, you may need to work with the Marketing teams of the retail stores by providing capital to promote the products you need to move faster. As a Merchandiser, you will also be the one to set pricing to maximize profits, manage performance ranges, planning overall promotions, and markdowns as needed. You are also acting as the gatekeeper of the brand - making sure that the lighting is perfect for these products, placements are correct, and the set-up in store is how the brand wants to portray the product. Lastly, you will work with the buying teams to accurately predict forecasting trends related to the products you are pushing.
