SEO & Paid Search

A Day in the Life

As a digital marketing specialist, you start your day by walking into your agency and greeting the rest of your account team. You work closely with your account executive who manages the relationships with the clients for whom you do digital marketing. You also work with a paid search specialist who formulates and optimizes all of the search campaigns you have running for any number of your clients.

As soon as you get into the office, your account executive calls an emergency meeting with your team - a client is threatening to leave unless we turn results around immediately. The traffic to their site has been down consistently, even with a search campaign running. Throwing money at the problem hasn’t worked, and you’re left scratching your head for ideas. You go back through the process and begin looking at their local listings to ensure their company name, address, and phone number are exactly the same across each network - and then you find it! Yelp and Yellow Pages have yet to update your client's new address information on their sites. All address information must be perfect across the web in order to fulfill the wants of a search engine.

You call Yelp and Yellow Pages and are on the phone for around 3 hours getting everything updated. You hope this will turn things around ASAP! You go back through the client's website to make sure each page is optimized properly for the search engines. You play around with some new keyword combinations, and remove some of your top 3 keywords that were used too liberally throughout the tertiary pages of the website.

Once you’re done with optimizing, you get to work on writing blog content for your clients. Once all 15 pieces of content are written, you begin mapping each blog article to the relevant keyword map to ensure that the correct keywords are used on each page of the website. What a day! You pack up and leave, excited for the challenges to come tomorrow.
