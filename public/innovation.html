Innovation Management

A Day in the Life

A day in the life in an Innovation role includes a lot of variety. It often includes talking to consumers to learn about them through primary research, which could include more general concept research all the way to sampling products and reacting to potential packaging. Not only will you have primary research to guide decisions, but you’ll also reference syndicated data, leveraging both to glean key insights that will help guide the innovation strategy. You might also work with R&D and Finance, prototyping products and understanding financial implications and costs. Next, you might work with the creative team on the vision for the packaging or early sales materials. Oftentimes, you’ll also work with outside agencies on upfront brainstorming and ideation sessions to think through white spaces and potential opportunities. You’re involved from the birth of an idea all the way until it’s ready to be launched in the marketplace.
