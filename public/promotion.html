Consumer Promotion

A Day in the Life

The day starts at a meeting with the innovation team to align on new products/offerings to launch in the next 2-3 years, followed by taking some time to work with your supply chain counterparts to manage inventory disruption or unanticipated demand. Next you might make a key decision on an item discontinuation based on some data leading you towards why the item isn’t performing to standards. Another task at hand is a meeting with your marketing insights/research team about consumer evaluation of a new product you’d like to explore. You could have a meeting with an agency or digital partner to provide scope or feedback for marketing content, ensuring that the voice and persona of the brand is coming to life in the intended fashion. Don’t forget the meeting with Finance about the profit margin and business performance analysis! Finally, you might end the day by working with the Sales team on customer presentations, requests, or supporting materials.
