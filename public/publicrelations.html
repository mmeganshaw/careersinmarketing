Public Relations & Advertising

A Day in the Life

Your day would likely begin by grabbing your cup of coffee, checking emails, and perusing media outlets for ads of your clients and their competitors. You visit a client’s office to go over the second round of logo iterations, giving them options and describing why each represents their brand in a different way. After they decide which they like best and provide some feedback, you take this information back to the office and share with your graphic designer to finalize. You then pop by the copywriting department to see how the language for the new social campaign series is going for another client. After another coffee, it’s time to meet with your creative team, putting the finishing touches on client #3’s project – a nationwide multi-media campaign you will be pitching this afternoon to really help them expand into new markets and boost sales. After your presentation, time to check emails and forward along some new creative briefs to your team, establishing timelines for drafts and the target date for a final piece. You will likely end your day reviewing all of your team’s active and upcoming projects, ensuring things are on schedule, and preparing for another productive day!
