Category Management

A Day in the Life

You might start your day by considering different shelving strategies and mocking up various shelf sets for specific categories. You would use category data, consumer research, retailer-specific data, and even primary research to recommend the best shelf set that would be most beneficial for the product or the brand. Next, you might assist with an analysis to prioritize what products should take up space on the shelf in the right mix that would most benefit the consumer, the retailer, and the manufacturer. You could then use data to understand category trends and key drivers, identify category opportunities, and monitor changes in consumer preferences. Analysis may be made into presentations for Sales support or guide brand/product decisions.
