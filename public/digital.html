A Day in the Life

The day starts with checking how much your paid search campaigns have spent the previous day, and you align on spend for today to align with your plan. You also schedule touch bases or pull reports to see how many impressions and clicks your display ads are getting. Think through with the team is you’re going after the right audiences and how to make the ads better and more engaging to consumers. You then meet with the internal digital marketing team to brainstorm new ideas for using new platforms like Pinterest, Snapchat and Instagram. New promotions will come through your email where you must prepare a plan for digital and determine goals. You may bring in a data analyst to give insights into where you’re returning more for your ad spend and where you are losing.

Other important tasks include: Writing ad copy for your paid search ads, coordinating with a 3rd party display vendor to serve your ads to people who have been in your store before or shopped your online store, report out return on ad spend to executives, and management of a daily spend tracker for all digital campaigns.
