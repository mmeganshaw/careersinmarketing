Creative Design

A Day in the Life

The day starts with a project status meeting to discuss current state and daily prioritization to align all team members and discuss any risks to the overall project timeline. Following which you join a client meeting where the client requests major changes to the creative assets delivered last week. Your project manager works with you to help prioritize current work and the rework requested by the client on today’s call. After returning to your desk, you begin to work through the revisions requested by the client and realize the requested changes are against brand guidelines. You work closely with the Creative Director and Project Manager on your account to discuss options and how to effectively communicate changes to the client. Then you run to a creative all hands meeting to discuss updates in your department over the last month. Your last meeting of the day is with the copywriter on your account to discuss the new creative brief and requirements you received yesterday for new digital assets coming down the pipeline. As the workday comes to a close you work to create a to-do list to help you prioritize efficiently the next day.
