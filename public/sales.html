Sales Management

A Day in the Life

The day starts by going over your calendar,prior notes,& To Do’s from the day before to get yourself into the right frame of mind. Secondly, you meet with the team to talk through your upcoming day and the prior day’s accomplishments. Following the agile meeting, it is time to grab a cup of coffee and begin the day. You look to do all email follow-ups in the morning because that is the time most individuals are prone to reviewing their email and potentially answering said email. Following sending emails it is time to do follow-up calls and make sure that current clients are taken care of and all needs are met. Throughout the rest of the day, you are emailing and cold calling prospects to set up meetings and understand their initiatives for the quarter or rest of year. You must stay on top of all activity-which means after calling, emailing, meeting etc.; you need to log that activity into Salesforce or another CRM platform. Lastly, depending on the company, end of day/ end of week, you meet with the team and talk through trials and tribulations you went through during the week along with high points you had during the week. You then create your To-Do list for the following day and more than anything MAKE A SALE!

    • - Maintain daily reporting within the current CRM (SalesForce, Hubspot, Excel etc.)
    • - Continue to train for outside sales/ inside sales management (understanding holding a book of business, follow up schedules, forecasting, and building pipeline volume)
    • - Research industries and companies within relevant industries to continuously identify new ICP’s (Ideal Customer Profiles) for New Business opportunities.
