Data Scientist

A Day in the Life

Start a day with checking the calendar – Yes! Both meetings are in the morning! I will have a block of time to work on the new data set I received from marketing.

Checking emails before going to my first meeting with tire engineers to talk through the best way to systematically collect the plant tire specs.

Running from one meeting to another to present the tire tread wear performance of a specific tire line extracted from the whole Goodyear retail stores.

All data crunching in the afternoon. It seems the new marketing data has many human errors that needed to be filtered out or corrected…. (data scientists spend most of their time cleaning and organizing the data instead of applying cool data models.)
