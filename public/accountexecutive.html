Account Executive

A Day in the Life

The day starts with checking unread emails to find the client sent a late night request to schedule a meeting to discuss a new project. Next, you’ll work to set up a meeting with all other account team members to discuss the client request and begin to brainstorm potential solutions and next steps. Following which, you have an upcoming meeting to discuss the current year’s goals and quotas with upper management to identify any gaps or risks to the overall company financials. Don’t forget last week’s negotiation meeting with another client to finalize budgets and contracts for a new scope of work. You’ll work to write a detailed statement of work that outlines the scope of work, applicable team members, timeline and invoice schedule to be delivered to the client for final approval and signature. Meanwhile, you’ll set up kick off meetings internally and externally to ramp up the team and make applicable introductions to the client. Before you leave for the day, you have a one-on-one meeting with the lead project manager to discuss a client compliant where you’ll work to provide a solution and mitigation plan. After wrapping up the last meeting of the day, you quickly grab your bag and run out the door to meet up with clients for drinks after work seeing as they are in town for a local conference.
